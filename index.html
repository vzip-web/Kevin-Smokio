Who is Smokio?

Born Kevin Silva on 26 October 1994 in Colombo, Kevin Smokio is an independent rap artist under Dope And Fresh Empire. Influenced by his father (musician M.C. Prasad Silva), he started at age 13. Joined Rusthiyadu Padanama (2012) as SMOKIO, then 44 Kalliya. Signed to Illinois Records via Iraj (2016) with breakout 'Mudukkuwen Eliyata'.

Overcame addiction after 2.5 years in rehab, returned 2020 with massive hit 'Liya Liyaa' (feat. Dilki Uresha, prod. Chamath Sangeeth). Now a youth role model inspiring millions with raw lyrics on street life, poverty, struggles, resilience, and social issues.

2026 highlights include record-breaking diss 'KUDDAH' (fastest SL hip-hop video to 1M views in 1 day) and collabs like NIRWAANE & RIP (REST IN POWER).
